LiveLarQ - Innovative Self-Cleaning Water Bottles

Eco-Friendly Hydration Solutions for Every Need

LARQ has made a significant impact on the hydration market with its range of self-cleaning, UV-C powered water bottles that provide safe, sustainable hydration wherever you go. LARQ’s collection of water bottles has been designed to cater to different lifestyles and hydration needs, offering products that are both functional and stylish. Whether you are looking for a lightweight bottle for travel or a durable insulated option for daily use, LARQ’s line of products offers a unique blend of convenience, safety, and environmental responsibility. Below, we explore LARQ’s primary products, their features, and how they address the diverse hydration needs of their users. The LARQ Purification Process: UV-C Technology for Cleaner Water Central to all LARQ products is the innovative UV-C LED technology that purifies water by neutralizing harmful bacteria and viruses. This purification process uses UV-C light, which penetrates the DNA of bacteria and viruses, rendering them inactive and ensuring that both the water and the bottle remain odor-free. With UV-C technology, LARQ bottles offer users the confidence of drinking clean water no matter where they are, without the need for filters or additional cleaning supplies. LARQ bottles are designed to self-clean every two hours, activating the UV-C light automatically to maintain cleanliness. This hands-free feature keeps your bottle fresh and ready to use, eliminating the hassle of frequent washing. It’s a solution that not only enhances convenience but also supports a healthier hydration habit.

The LARQ Product Range: Tailored for Every Lifestyle

LARQ Bottle PureVis

The LARQ Bottle PureVis is LARQ’s flagship product, featuring advanced UV-C LED technology within a double-walled, insulated stainless steel design. This bottle is perfect for those who need an insulated bottle to keep their water cold for up to 24 hours or hot for up to 12 hours. With a sleek, modern look and various color options, the PureVis is both functional and visually appealing, making it suitable for use at work, the gym, or on outdoor adventures.

LARQ Bottle PureVis

The LARQ Bottle Movement is a lightweight, non-insulated version designed for users who prioritize portability. This model is ideal for travel, outdoor activities, and workouts, as it provides all the benefits of LARQ’s UV-C purification in a lighter, more compact form. Equipped with a silicone sleeve for added grip and durability, the Movement model is built for users who are always on the move.

LARQ Pitcher

The LARQ Pitcher brings the brand’s purification technology to home use, offering a convenient way to enjoy purified water from the comfort of your kitchen. Unlike the portable LARQ bottles, the Pitcher uses an advanced filtration system that combines UV-C LED purification with an innovative filter to remove impurities commonly found in tap water. This dual-action system makes it a unique choice for families or individuals who prioritize safe drinking water at home.
