Meet Live LarQ



Our Story and Our Commitment to Clean, Sustainable Hydration

LARQ’s story began with Justin Wang, who wanted to address two major issues: the environmental impact of single-use plastic bottles and the challenge of maintaining a clean, odor-free water bottle. Traveling frequently, Justin noticed the reliance on disposable plastic bottles for convenience, but he knew there had to be a more sustainable solution. He recognized the need for a reusable water bottle that was easy to maintain, hygienic, and effective at keeping water clean. This idea led to the creation of LARQ, a brand committed to merging technology and sustainability to provide consumers with a safe, reusable option for drinking water. To achieve this goal, LARQ harnessed UV-C LED technology—a type of light that eliminates up to 99.9% of bacteria and viruses—within a sleek, portable bottle. This innovation offered a truly self-cleaning solution, one that did not require constant washing or filters, and one that could deliver fresh, clean water to users worldwide.

The Mission: Clean, Convenient, and Sustainable Hydration for All

LARQ’s mission is straightforward yet ambitious: to provide consumers with a healthier way to hydrate and to inspire a movement toward reducing plastic waste. The team at LARQ believes that clean water is a basic necessity and that sustainability is an essential part of global health. Every LARQ product reflects these values, ensuring users don’t have to compromise between convenience and environmental responsibility. From the beginning, LARQ’s approach has centered on creating a product that can benefit both individuals and the planet. By making clean water accessible without disposable plastic bottles, LARQ contributes to the reduction of plastic waste in oceans and landfills. Each LARQ bottle is crafted with premium materials designed to last, giving users an option that aligns with their values and commitment to sustainability.

LARQ’s Groundbreaking Technology: UV-C LED Purification

A core component of LARQ’s innovation is its use of UV-C LED technology, a scientifically backed method for water purification. This technology, which is often used in medical and scientific environments, harnesses the power of UV-C light to break down the DNA of bacteria and viruses, rendering them inactive. In LARQ bottles, the UV-C light operates at the push of a button, eliminating contaminants within seconds.

What makes LARQ’s technology unique is its automatic self-cleaning feature, which activates every two hours to ensure that both the water and the bottle remain fresh. This built-in cycle keeps the bottle odor-free, preventing the buildup of bacteria that often plagues traditional reusable bottles. As a result, LARQ users enjoy the peace of mind that their water remains pure and their bottle hygienic.
